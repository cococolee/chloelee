About me



Hi! I'm Chloe, a second-year student pursuing Global Business and Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo.


Beyond the textbooks, you'll often find me out and about, exploring the city, indulging in good food, capturing moments through my lens, breaking a sweat at the gym, and embracing what life has to offer!


What truly gets my creative juices flowing is the captivating realm of UX/UI and graphic design. The way these fields seamlessly blend technology and aesthetics has me hooked. I'm on a mission to soak up every bit of knowledge, embracing the challenges and trends that come my way.


I'm fueled by the excitement of collaborating with like-minded individuals and organizations. My goal? To weave my skills and creativity into projects that leave a mark on the industry. So, if you're all about pushing boundaries and making an impact in the digital realm, let's connect and create something extraordinary together!
